Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Javelina Hideout located?

We are located in the Big Bend region of West Texas, near Big Bend National Park. Our secluded property offers stunning desert views and easy access to the park's natural wonders.

Is the property off-grid?

Yes, Javelina Hideout is completely off-grid, offering a unique desert experience with solar power and complete privacy. Enjoy the tranquility of the desert while still having modern comforts.

How do I make a reservation?

Click the RESERVE button at the top of the page to check availability and book your stay through our booking system.

What amenities are available?

The hideout features both an east and west porch for enjoying sunrise and sunset views, a firepit for evening relaxation, and all the essentials for a comfortable desert retreat.

What is the best time to visit?

The desert is beautiful year-round, but spring and fall offer the most comfortable temperatures. Winter can be chilly at night, and summer days are hot—but the stargazing is spectacular any time of year.

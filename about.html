Frequently Asked Questions

How far are you from the park and other areas of interest?

  • 25 miles to the west entrance station of Big Bend National Park (roughly 30 minutes)
  • 26 miles to Terlingua Ghost Town (roughly 31 minutes)
  • 12 miles to Terlingua Ranch Lodge (roughly 20 minutes)
  • 21 miles to the nearest gas station in Study Butte (roughly 25 minutes)

Do you allow dogs?

We love pets, but Javelina Hideout does not allow pets. Big Bend National Park's trails and backcountry areas do not allow dogs (NPS pet rules), and we don't want guests to risk breaking the rules or missing out on park adventures. Even short outings can turn into longer adventures with hikes, traffic, or simply enjoying the park, making it challenging to care for a pet. Keeping JH pet-free ensures a safe, stress-free, and relaxing experience for all guests.

Can I have a campfire?

Yes, you can have a campfire with the following in mind:

  • You will need provide your own firewood. It's best to bring it from home, as wood is a rare resource in the desert and very expensive. It can be purchased in town, but it's very pricey.
  • You will need to keep your campfire within the provided fire ring.
  • Please be sure that ALL coals are extinguished prior to retiring for the evening. If the wind picks up (which it frequently does), this can disperse hot coals into the desert and start a fire.

Is there WIFI?

Yes, we have fiber optic internet with very fast upload/download speeds and over 99% uptime. We also have a dedicated desk with a large monitor for guest use.

Can I use Google Maps to navigate to the property?

We have recently been added to Google Maps, so you should be able to navigate this way. Keep in mind there isn't much cell service in the area, so navigate early and understand that you won't be able to reroute if you get off course.

Do I need a 4WD to get to the property?

No you do not need a 4WD to get to the property. Any standard sized sedan will get you there! We have 1 mile of all weather dirt road, so rain is not problem either.

Will my cellphone work at the property?

No, we do not have cell at the property, but our super fast WiFi makes phonecalls seamless.

Do you have air conditioning with your solar setup?

Yes! We have ample power for air conditioning the main home and also in the bunkhouse. We have a powerful system that will allow you to run the AC at night as well. If you keep the temps at 74 degrees, you should be good all night.

Are you kid friendly?

Yes! We are kid friendly. The older kids love our bunkhouse - they can have their own space. They also love the trail around the property - lots of fun things to be found on the trail.

Does the bunkhouse get rented separately?

No, the bunkhouse is never rented out separately. The place is always entirely yours.

Do you have any nearby neighbors?

Javelina Hideout is very private. We are on 25 acres of land. There are homes on our block, but nothing adjacent to our property. You will have lots of privacy, peace and quiet.
